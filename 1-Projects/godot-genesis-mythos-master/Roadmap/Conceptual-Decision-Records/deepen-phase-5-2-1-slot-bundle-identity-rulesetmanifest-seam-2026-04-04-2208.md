## Summary

Minted tertiary **5.2.1** with **slot-kind taxonomy** (`generator_slot` / `event_slot` / `style_slot`), **bundle documentation identity** fields, and **explicit mapping tables** tying bundle seams to **RulesetManifest** + **5.1.1** **3.4.1** admission — preserving a single seam authority and strengthening **GWT-5.2.1-A–K** evidence columns vs **GWT-5.2** and **GWT-5.1.1**.

## PMG alignment

Keeps ecosystem remix **documentation** replay-stable under **ruleset pins** while **consuming** Phase **5.1** manifest and Phase **3.4.1** seam catalog — no duplicate consumer truth — aligned with the master goal’s rules-engine + community extensibility story.

## Alternatives and tradeoffs

| Alternative | Upside | Downide | Why not chosen |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Single merged “swap envelope”** in manifest | One artifact | Collides with **5.1.1** minimal manifest story; premature schema lock | Deferred — **5.2** open question carries forward |
| **Bundle-defined SeamIds** | Faster author iteration | Violates **3.4.1** authority | Rejected |
| **Skip mapping tables** | Shorter note | Weak **GWT** traceability | Rejected per operator **user_guidance** |
