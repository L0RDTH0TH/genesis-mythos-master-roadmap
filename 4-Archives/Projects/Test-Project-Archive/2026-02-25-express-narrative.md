## What am I thinking?

The Second Brain workflow has four main pipelines: ingest (capture and organize), distill (progressive summarization), archive (move completed items), and express (turn notes into shareable output). Each pipeline has a clear trigger phrase and confidence gates. Backup and snapshot before destructive steps keep the vault safe.

## What does this seem to mean?

It means we can run small batches for empirical validation: distill 5–10 notes, archive 5–15 completed ones, express 1–3 narrative notes. The key is to run them in a test project first and verify logs and snapshots.
