## Phase 1 — Game Grid

Fixed grid (e.g. 20×20 cells), wrap-around walls or solid walls option. This phase nails down the board size, coordinate system, and whether the snake wraps around at the edges or dies on impact, since that choice affects both difficulty and feel.
