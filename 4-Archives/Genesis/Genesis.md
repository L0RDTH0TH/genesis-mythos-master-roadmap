# Genesis

## TL;DR

_Open-source Godot-based 3D first-person VTT: player- and DM-owned world, SVG→Terrain3D maps, Free5e/A5e mechanics, authoritative DM, procedural gen as background. Time-bound to playable MVP (~3–4 months)._

## Goal / Endpoint

Ship a playable alpha: 1 DM + up to 5 players, first-person open-world exploration + D&D-style turn-based combat, 2D SVG map maker feeding editable 3D Terrain3D maps, Free5e (optional A5e) for rules and character/combat.
