# Master goal for Genesis — 3D VTT vision

## TL;DR

Build an open-source Godot-based 3D first-person VTT: player- and DM-owned world, SVG→Terrain3D maps, Free5e/A5e mechanics, authoritative DM. Proc gen is background/filler only; world and lore are built by players & DM together.

## Master goal

Build a 3D first-person virtual tabletop RPG platform (heavily inspired by D&D) where:

- **World ownership**: Lore, history, factions, locations are primarily player- and DM-generated.
- **Proc gen**: Exists only as filler / inspiration / examples — never the final authority.
- **Collaborative editing**: DM and players shape and edit the world in real time during play.
- **Core loop**: First-person open-world exploration + classic D&D-style turn-based combat.
- **Scale**: 1 Dungeon Master (authoritative host) + up to 5 players.
- **Mechanics**: User-guided proc gen, world presets by theme, custom rule tweaking, character creation/customization via Free5e (optionally A5e).
- **Maps**: 2D SVG-based map maker (infinite zoom) feeding editable 3D maps (Terrain3D).
- **Content-agnostic**: No built-in art or story — 100% user responsibility.

**One sentence:** Open-source Godot-based 3D first-person VTT where the living world and its lore are built and owned by players & DM together, proc gen demoted to background noise, using SVG→Terrain3D maps, Free5e/A5e mechanics, and authoritative DM control.
