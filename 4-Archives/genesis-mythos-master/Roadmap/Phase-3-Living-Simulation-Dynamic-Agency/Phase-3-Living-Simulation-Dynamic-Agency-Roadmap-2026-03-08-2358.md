## Phase 3 — Living Simulation and Dynamic Agency

Bring the world to life with persistent, balanced elements. Build the tick-based simulation layer: weather cycles, NPC agendas, faction interactions, persistent state changes. Incorporate DM overwrites: real-time tweaks vs deliberate re-generation for major changes. Layer vitality features: ambient surprises, consequence graphs, evolution mechanics. Decouple simulation from visuals for lightweight previews while keeping full-session fluidity.
