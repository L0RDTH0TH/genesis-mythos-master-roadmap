# OG Minecraft Systems (Alpha/Beta Era Focus)

## 1. World Generation
Procedural terrain (biomes, caves, ores), infinite world chunks, seed-based.

## 2. Blocks & Block States
Basic blocks (dirt, stone, wood), break/place mechanics, light levels.

## 3. Player Controls & Inventory
Movement (walk, jump, swim), hotbar, inventory UI, crafting grid.

## 4. Tools & Items
Crafting recipes, durability, mining speeds, drops.

## 5. Mobs & Entities
Passive/hostile mobs (zombies, cows), spawning, AI pathing, drops.

## 6. Combat & Health
Damage system, hunger bar, regeneration, death/respawn.

## 7. Redstone & Circuits
Wires, torches, pistons, basic logic gates.

## 8. Fluids & Physics
Water/lava flow, gravity (sand/gravel), fire spread.

## 9. Day/Night Cycle & Weather
Time of day, mob spawn rates at night, rain/snow.

## 10. Multiplayer Basics (Optional)
LAN/server mode, player syncing, chat.

## 11. Rendering & Graphics
Blocky voxels, simple textures, fog, skybox.
