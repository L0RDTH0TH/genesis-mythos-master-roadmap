## Other Sourcebooks (SCAG, Spelljammer, Theros, MOoT, WBtW, etc.)

- **Air Genasi**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Dexterity
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Unending Breath, Levitate (once per long rest)

- **Earth Genasi**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Strength
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Merge with Stone (Pass without trace once per long rest), Earth Walk (ignore difficult terrain on stone/earth)

- **Fire Genasi**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Intelligence
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Produce Flame cantrip, Fire Resistance

- **Water Genasi**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 30 ft. (Swim 30 ft.)
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Amphibious, Acid Resistance, Swim proficiency

- **Plasmoid**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 to another ability of choice
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Amorphous Form (squeeze through tight spaces), Pseudopod (natural attack), Adhesive Body (climb walls)

- **Owlin**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 30 ft. (Fly 30 ft.)
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Silent Feathers (Stealth advantage), Keen Senses (Perception proficiency)

- **Harengon**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Lucky Footwork (avoid attacks), Rabbit Hop (bonus movement), Keen Senses (Perception proficiency)

- **Simic Hybrid**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 to another ability of choice
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Amphibious, Hybrid Adaptation (choose climbing/swimming/acid resistance), Grappling Appendages (extra limbs for attacks or grapples)

- **Fairy**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
  - **Speed:** 30 ft. (Fly 30 ft.)
  - **Key Traits:** Fey Magic (Druidcraft cantrip), Flight, Superior Dexterity, Tiny Size

- **Thri-kreen**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 35 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Chameleon Carapace (Stealth), Multi-Limbed (extra natural attacks), Jumping (long jump bonus)

- **Centaur**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 40 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Charge, Hooves (natural weapon), Equine Build, Skill Proficiencies (Nature & Survival)

- **Lizardfolk**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Strength
  - **Speed:** 30 ft. (Swim 30 ft.)
  - **Key Traits:** Bite (natural weapon), Cunning Artisan, Hold Breath, Hunter’s Lore

- **Leonin**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Daunting Roar, Claws, Hunter’s Instinct, Keen Smell

- **Minotaur**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Horn Attack, Goring Rush, Hammering Horns, Labyrinthine Recall

- **Giff**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Martial Training, Breath Weapon Resistance, Firearms proficiency (setting-specific)

- **Vedalken**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Intelligence, +1 Wisdom
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Vedalken Dispassion (advantage on all Int, Wis, Cha saves vs. magic), Tireless Precision (Int checks), Partially Amphibious

- **Kobold**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, -2 Strength
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Grovel/Cower/Beg (help allies), Pack Tactics

- **Goblinoids** *(Goblin, Hobgoblin, Bugbear included in Volo’s; duplicates skipped)*

- **Tabaxi, Yuan-ti, Aasimar** *(already in Volo’s; duplicates skipped)*

- **Other minor/optional races from Spelljammer & WBtW**
  - **Astral Elf** *(Spelljammer)*: +2 Dex, +1 Int, Darkvision, Trance, Astral Step
  - **Hadozee** *(Spelljammer)*: +2 Dex, +1 Con, Climb and Glide, Darkvision, Nautical Adaptation
  - **Kender** *(optional classic)*: +2 Dex, +1 Cha, Fearless, Nimble, Lucky
