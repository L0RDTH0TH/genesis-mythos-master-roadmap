## Player’s Handbook (PHB)

- **Dragonborn**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Charisma
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Breath Weapon, Damage Resistance (based on draconic ancestry)

- **Dwarf**
  - **Hill Dwarf**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +1 Wisdom
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Dwarven Resilience, Dwarven Combat Training, Tool Proficiency
  - **Mountain Dwarf**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Constitution, +2 Strength
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Dwarven Resilience, Dwarven Combat Training, Tool Proficiency, Armor Proficiency

- **Elf**
  - **High Elf**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Intelligence
    - **Speed:** 30 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry, Trance, Cantrip
  - **Wood Elf**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Wisdom
    - **Speed:** 35 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Keen Senses, Fey Ancestry, Trance, Mask of the Wild
  - **Drow**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
    - **Speed:** 30 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Trance, Drow Magic, Sunlight Sensitivity

- **Halfling**
  - **Lightfoot**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Charisma
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Lucky, Brave, Halfling Nimbleness
  - **Stout**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Dexterity, +1 Constitution
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Lucky, Brave, Stout Resilience

- **Human**
  - **Standard Human**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +1 to all six abilities
    - **Speed:** 30 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** None (base features)
  - **Variant Human**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +1 to two abilities of choice
    - **Speed:** 30 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Bonus Feat, Extra Skill

- **Tiefling**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Hellish Resistance, Infernal Legacy

- **Gnome**
  - **Forest Gnome**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Intelligence, +1 Dexterity
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Gnome Cunning, Natural Illusionist, Speak with Small Beasts
  - **Rock Gnome**
    - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Intelligence, +1 Constitution
    - **Speed:** 25 ft.
    - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Gnome Cunning, Artificer’s Lore, Tinker

- **Half-Elf**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Charisma, +1 to two other abilities
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Skill Versatility

- **Half-Orc**
  - **Ability Score Increase:** +2 Strength, +1 Constitution
  - **Speed:** 30 ft.
  - **Key Traits:** Darkvision, Menacing, Relentless Endurance, Savage Attacks
