## Archetype Themes at a Glance

### Identity and Self
- **The Self**: Unity, balance, wholeness
- **The Persona**: Social masks, adaptability
- **The Shadow**: Hidden, repressed aspects

### Transformation and Balance
- **The Anima/Animus**: Contrasexual aspects, balance
- **The Lover**: Passion, connection
- **The Maiden**: Purity, potential

### Guidance and Authority
- **The Wise Old Man/Woman**: Knowledge, insight
- **The Great Mother**: Nurturing or destructive maternal force
- **The Father**: Authority, protection
- **The Ruler**: Leadership, order
- **The Sage**: Truth-seeking, philosophy

### Rebellion and Change
- **The Hero**: Overcoming challenges
- **The Child**: Innocence, new beginnings
- **The Trickster**: Cunning, disruption
- **The Rebel**: Defiance, freedom
- **The Creator**: Innovation, artistry
- **The Magician**: Transformation, spiritual power
